# 💡  KEY INSIGHTS FOR STRATEGIC DECISIONS

# Insight 1: Stepped Care is Non-Negotiable

Every evidence-based system globally uses stepped care. Feel Again cannot succeed with "one size fits all" approach. Must implement:

**Level 1 (Digital Self-Help):** WHO Self-Help Plus через chatbot, without або with minimal human guidance. Cost: minimal. Reaches: thousands.  
**Level 2 (Brief Structured Support):** PM+ delivered by briefly-trained helpers (може бути peer supporters, не обов'язково psychologists). Cost: low. Reaches: hundreds.  
**Level 3 (Standard Psychological Therapy):** Current Feel Again model \- verified psychologists delivering CBT, IPT та інші evidence-based therapies. Cost: moderate. Reaches: moderate numbers.  
**Level 4 (Specialized/Intensive Care):** Complex PTSD, comorbidities, VR-assisted therapy, psychiatrist involvement. Cost: high. Reaches: smaller numbers but highest need.

*Критично: people can step UP або step DOWN based on progress. This is efficient resource allocation and clinically appropriate.*

# Insight 2: Non-Specialist Delivery is Scalable, Specialists are Bottleneck

**WHO mhGAP та IAPT models prove:** properly trained, supervised non-specialists can deliver effective interventions. Feel Again має розширити beyond лише licensed psychologists:  
Potential non-specialist roles:

**E-helpers (для digital guided self-help)**

* Psychological Well-being Practitioners (після structured training)

* Peer supporters (people з lived experience warfare trauma, trained в PM+)

* Community health workers (базова psychoeducation та PFA)

Це не заміна psychologists \- це expanding access, reserving психологів for moderate-to-severe cases.

# Insight 3: VR/AR не для всіх \- Game-Changer для Specific Subgroup

VR therapy має compelling evidence але:

* Expensive (hardware, software, trained therapists)  
* Not needed для всіх PTSD cases (багато respond до традиційного PE або CPT)  
* Most effective for: treatment-resistant PTSD, patients unable engage imagination, "digital native" younger veterans

**Strategic recommendation:** VR як specialized Level 4 intervention, not universal. Partner з існуючими VR centers (військові rehabilitation facilities?) rather than building in-house initially.

# Insight 4: Cultural Adaptation не Optional

Every WHO intervention emphasizes cultural adaptation. Feel Again має Ukrainian-specific versions:

Language (Ukrainian, можливо Russian для деяких регіонів)  
Examples у chatbot narratives (Ukrainian cities, names, cultural references)  
Coping strategies aligned з Ukrainian cultural norms  
Integration з Ukrainian traditional support systems (extended family, community networks, faith communities)

Generic translation insufficient \- needs cultural pilot testing з Ukrainian users.

# Insight 5: Data Transparency \+ Blockchain \= Unprecedented Opportunity

No other MHPSS platform globally має blockchain-verified transparency. This is Feel Again's unique competitive advantage:

Leverage для stakeholders:

* Donors: every dollar tracked від P2P до session payment  
* Government: real-time population mental health data (anonymous)  
* Researchers: largest de-identified dataset Ukrainian war trauma  
* Beneficiaries: control over own data, portable records

But: requires bulletproof data security, GDPR compliance, ethical oversight. One breach destroys trust permanently.

# Key Decision Matrix for Feel Again Stepped Care:

***Level 1 (Digital Self-Help):***

* *WHO Self-Help Plus (chatbot-delivered)*  
* *Psychoeducation materials*  
* *PFA-guided initial contact*

***Level 2 (Guided Low-Intensity):***

* ***WET** as primary option (5 sessions, highest completion rate)*  
* *PM+ as alternative (5 sessions, problem-solving focus)*  
* *Delivered by trained e-helpers/peer supporters*

***Level 3 (Standard Psychological Therapy):***

* ***CPT** as first-line (strongest military evidence, 12 sessions)*  
* ***NET** for multiple/complex trauma (especially refugees/IDPs, 8-10 sessions)*  
* *EMDR as alternative (for patients preferring no homework)*

***Level 4 (Specialized/Intensive):***

* ***VR-assisted Prolonged Exposure** (BRAVEMIND partnership з Skip)*  
* *Variable-length CPT (for treatment-resistant cases, up to 18 sessions)*  
* *Combination therapy (VR \+ pharmacotherapy for comorbidities)*
